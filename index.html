Pre-Design Thinking

Startup: Inedit(Brandazine)
Fashion retail service for content creators. It is an exclusive fashion rental service for creators on Instagram and Youtube. Creators pay a monthly subscription to gain unlimited access to fashion products and the opportunity to sell their content to brands.
Link: https://www.ycombinator.com/companies/inedit-brandazine
Interface: Mobile
Users: content creators and fashion brands
To connect fashion brands to content creators in a streamlined manner.

Critique Feedback:

  • There is no back arrow from the signup screen just in case you click the wrong button on the landing screen.
  • Some of the fonts are difficult to read.
  • Good contrast color for subscription, but contraction between gold and platinum is not distinct. Gold stands out more than platinum due to the coloring.
  • Hesitant to sign up immediately, so implement free trial, so user is able to see the mechnics of the application before subscribing
  • Confusion about the meaning of the checkmarks on the profile page
  • The icons in the bottom navigation bar are intuitive for the most part, but text would help for some people who don't understand.
